== Resources

Evidence sources and supporting materials used to understand the domain and justify requirements. For Milestone 1, we list only the most important sources; extended notes are linked.

#These resources represent inputs to the acquisition process, serving as raw domain evidence before being analyzed during elicitation and requirements definition.#

=== External sources (selected)
* Household food waste research: supports the need for better visibility and expiration awareness in student households.
* Personal Information Management (PIM) research: supports the risk that high-effort or overly frequent updates lead to abandonment and notification fatigue.
* Household finance / budgeting research (general): supports the importance of spending awareness for budget-constrained students.
