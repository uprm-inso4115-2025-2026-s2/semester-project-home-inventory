== Resources

This subsection lists supporting materials and evidence sources used during requirements acquisition. These resources support the acquisition process, but they are not a substitute for the questionnaire, interview protocol, or participant-coded raw evidence.

=== External Sources (selected)
* Household food waste research: supports the importance of visibility and expiration awareness in student households.
* Personal Information Management (PIM) research: supports the risk that high-effort updates and excessive reminders lead to abandonment.
* Household finance and budgeting research: supports the importance of spending awareness for budget-constrained students.
