=== Objective

Formally document the internal validation logic that prevents unintended duplicate entries before they are stored in the system.

Applies to: Team 1 (Core Inventory) and Team 3 (Sharing & Budget).

Scope: prevention logic that occurs BEFORE inserting new inventory records, BEFORE creating new Product definitions, and BEFORE committing Spend Events.

Out of scope: post-insertion duplicate correction; budget edge-case conflict resolution (see: Edge Cases (Duplicates/Budget)).

=== Prevention Principles (Quick Reference)

. Duplicate prevention must occur before database insertion.
. Barcode match is authoritative  it overrides name/brand differences.
. Product definitions must be normalized before comparison.
. Near-duplicates require explicit user confirmation before reuse.
. Spend Events must only be created after duplicate resolution is finalized.
. Prevention logic must be deterministic and reproducible.
. No two Products may share an identical barcode (enforced at DB level).
. Duplicate prevention decisions must be auditable.
