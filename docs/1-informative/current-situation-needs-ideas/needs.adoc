== Needs

The needs below describe what students living independently and their roommates must be able to accomplish to manage household essentials reliably. These needs are expressed as outcomes and capabilities (what stakeholders need to achieve), independent of any specific system-to-be.

*Needs by actor group*

=== Actor group: Students and young adults
* Need N1: Minimize the effort and time required to keep track of household items
* Need N2: Maintain an accurate understanding of what items are available as they are consumed
* Need N3: Determine whether an item is already available before making a purchase

=== Actor group: Roommates / Cohabitants
* Need N4: Maintain a shared understanding of what items are available to the household
* Need N5: Coordinate responsibility for replenishing shared items to avoid duplicate or missed purchases

=== Prioritized needs
[cols="1,4,2,3",options="header"]
|===
|ID |Need statement |Priority |Rationale

|N1 |Users must be able to record items in seconds
|High
|Addresses high-friction item entry reported in user feedback

|N2 |Users must maintain an up-to-date inventory with low ongoing effort
|High
|Mitigates abandonment caused by needing high maintainance

|N3 |Users must quickly verify existing items before purchasing
|High
|Reduces duplicate purchases and wasted spending

|N4 |Household members must share inventory visibility
|Medium
|Improves coordination in shared living environments

|N5 |Household members must coordinate replenishment of shared items to avoid duplicate or missed purchases
|Medium
|Reduces miscommunication and duplicate spending in shared living environments
|===

=== Quality Needs (Non-Functional)

* Usability: Household members need to update item tracking with minimal effort and without training.
* Reliability: Household members need to trust that the tracked inventory matches what is actually available.
* Performance: Household members need inventory information quickly enough to use it while shopping or planning.
* Availability: Household members need tracking to remain usable even when connectivity is unreliable.
* Security/Privacy: Household members need confidence that household data is only visible to intended people.
* Scalability: Household members need the experience to remain smooth as more items, households, and members are added.
