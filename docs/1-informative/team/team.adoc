== Team

For Milestone 1, the Digital Home Inventory team is organized into feature-based groups to support parallel development: Core Inventory, Alerts & Restock, Sharing & Budget, and Reports & Insights; plus a cross-functional Design Team, a Documentation Team, and two Project Managers. In this documentation, all team members are considered partners because they have a direct stake in and responsibility for Milestone 1 outcomes (planning, design, implementation, testing, and documentation), not merely being end users of the system-to-be. No external partners have been identified for this milestone; if any arise, their roles and responsibilities will be documented in this section. Potential external partners may include a faculty mentor, domain informants (students interviewed), or external technical advisors; if involved beyond feedback (e.g., providing recurring reviews, requirements validation, or architectural guidance), their responsibilities and contributions will be recorded here. The lists are presented in alphabetical order and indicate each group’s Team Lead. The Tasks column lists associated GitHub issue numbers (and may later include short notes about responsibilities as work completes).
