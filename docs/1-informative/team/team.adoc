== Team

For Milestone 1, the Digital Home Inventory team is organized into feature-based groups to support parallel development: Core Inventory, Alerts & Restock, Sharing & Budget, and Reports & Insights; plus a cross-functional Design Team, a Documentation Team, and two Project Managers. In this context, all team members are considered partners because each member contributes directly to Milestone 1 deliverables. No external partners have been identified for this milestone; if any arise, their roles and responsibilities will be documented in this section. The lists are presented in alphabetical order and indicate each group’s Team Lead. The Tasks column is reserved for recording completed issues and responsibilities during the milestone.
