== Team

For Milestone 2, the Digital Home Inventory team is organized into two project managers, a cross-functional Documentation Team, and four feature teams: Core Inventory, Alerts & Restock, Sharing & Budget, and Reports & Insights. This structure reflects how the milestone work is actually distributed in the tables below: each feature team is responsible for advancing its area through coordinated design, backend, documentation, testing, and implementation work, while the Documentation Team supports milestone-wide consistency, integration of changes, and delivery of the official project documentation. All listed team members are considered partners because they have direct responsibility for shaping Milestone 2 outcomes, including analysis, requirements refinement, implementation, validation and verification, and documentation. They therefore contribute to project decisions and deliverables rather than participating only as future end users of the system-to-be. No external participants are treated as formal partners for this milestone, since interviewed students and other domain informants mainly provide evidence and feedback, but do not hold recurring responsibility for project decisions, implementation, or milestone deliverables. If an external participant later takes on an ongoing role such as recurring requirements validation, architectural review, or milestone-level approval support, that role and its responsibilities will be documented here. The tables below identify each group and team lead, and the Tasks column records the associated GitHub issues used to trace each member’s contributions.
