
== Duplicate Warnings Icon
Author: Nataliavera6
--
This icon was necessary to provide a simple and effective way to notify users of duplicate items.
The number 2 in the icon indicates that there are two items that are duplicates as well as the two identical boxes. 

--
= Risks
- Users may not understand the meaning of the icon and may ignore it, leading to confusion and potential errors in their inventory management.
- Users may find the icon visually unappealing or distracting, which could negatively impact their user

= Benefits & Mitigations
- The number 2 in the icon provides a clear indication of the number of duplicate items, making it easier for users to identify and address the issue.
- The simple design of the icon helps to minimize visual clutter and allows users to quickly understand its purpose.
