== 2.1.2 Terminology

[abstract]
This section contains the domain`s ubiquitous language. Here we designate our preferred terms for entities, rules, events and actions from the domain. These were compiled from interviews with domain stakeholders, experts and general knowledge.

[cols="^,^,4,3",options="header"]
|===
|Term |Type |Definition |Synonyms / notes
| Home  | Entity | A physical space where people can, or do, live in | Household, Property, House, Place
| Item | Entity | A household item that is commonly restocked such as milk, toilet paper, dishwashing soap, etc. | Stock
| Room | Entity | A specific area within a Home where Items can be stored or located such as kitchen, bedroom, garage | StorageArea 
| Inventory | Entity | The list of available Items in a Home | List
| Item Location | Entity | The association between an Item and the Room where it is stored | Placement, StorageSpot
| Occupant | Entity | A person living in a Home and who should have access to its Inventory | Resident, Roommate, Manager, Owner, HouseholdMember, Homeowner, User
| Category | Entity | A grouping label used to organize Items by type or purpose such as food, cleaning, electronics |
| Expiration | Event | The point in time after which an Item is no longer usable | 
| Expiration Date | Rule | The specific date associated with an Item`s Expiration | BestByDate, UseByDate
| Expired Item | Event | An event triggered when an Item passes its Expiration Date | Spoiled, OutOfDate
| Item Status | Rule | The current state of an Item in the Inventory (e.g., available, low stock, expired) | AvailabilityState
| Quantity | Rule | The measurable amount of an Item available in the Inventory | Count, Level, Amount
| Unit | Rule | The measurement type used for an Item`s Quantity (e.g., pieces, liters, pounds) | MeasurementUnit
| Consumption | Event | The act of using or reducing an Item`s Quantity over time | Usage, Depletion
| Purchase | Action | The act of buying an Item to increase its Quantity in the Inventory | Restock, Buy
| Shopping List | Entity | A temporary list of Items that need to be purchased soon | GroceryList, PurchaseList
| Supplier | Entity | A store or provider where an Item can be purchased or restocked from | Store, Vendor
| Restock Threshold | Rule | The minimum Quantity at which an Item should be replenished | MinimumStock, LowStockLimit
| Restock Request | Action | A request created to indicate an Item needs to be purchased or refilled | ShoppingTask, RefillRequest
|===

=== Open questions / disputed terms
- The term currently used for Occupant is still in debate, this decision is tricky because we want this to encompass a single home resident, a roommate, and an owner of multiple properties such as an AirBnB manager.
