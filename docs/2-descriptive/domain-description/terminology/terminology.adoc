== Terminology

This section contains the domain`s ubiquitous language. Here we designate our preferred terms for entities, rules, events and actions from the domain. These were compiled from interviews with domain stakeholders, experts and general knowledge.

[cols="^,^,4,3",options="header"]
|===
|Term |Type |Definition |Synonyms / notes
| Home  | Entity | A physical space where people can, or do, live in | Household, Property, House, Place
| Household | Entity | An inventory group that represents a Home and its associated Inventory and Stakeholders | Home, Property, House, Place
| Product | Entity | A household item that is commonly restocked such as milk, toilet paper, dishwashing soap, etc. | 
| Stock | Entity | An instance of a Product, the Product entity holds a list of Stock containing each of the inidividual items bought such as two cartons of milk each with its specific expiration date and location, etc. | Item, Stock Entry
| Room | Entity | A specific area within a Home where Stock can be stored or located such as kitchen, bedroom, garage | StorageArea 
| Inventory | Entity | The list of available Products in a Home | List
| Stock Location | Entity | The association between Stock and the Room where it is stored | Placement, StorageSpot
| Stakeholder | Entity | A person that has a vested interest in a Home and its Inventory, Stakeholders have specific roles depicting their involvement | Resident, Roommate, Manager, Owner, HouseholdMember, Homeowner, User
| Role | Rule | The access control concept that dictates how much of a Home and an Inventory a Stakeholder can change |
| Owner | Entity | A Stakeholder Role that has the utmost access and permission to make changes to their Homes and Inventories | Manager, Homeowner
| Member | Entity | A Stakeholder Role that describes a Stakeholder who lives in a Home and who should have access to its Inventory | Resident, Roommate, HouseholdMember
| Membership | Rule | The association between a Stakeholder and a Household that defines their Role and access to the Household`s Inventory |
| Guest | Entity | A Stakeholder Role that should have view-only access to their Inventory and Home | 
| Invite | Entity | A pending membership access request to a Household that has not yet been accepted | PendingInvite
| Category | Entity | A grouping label used to organize Products by type or purpose such as food, cleaning, electronics |
| Expiration | Event | The point in time after which Stock is no longer usable | 
| Expiration Date | Rule | The specific date associated with Stock`s Expiration | BestByDate, UseByDate
| Expired Stock | Event | An event triggered when Stock passes its Expiration Date | Spoiled, OutOfDate
| Stock Status | Rule | The current state of Stock in the Inventory (e.g., available, low stock, expired) | AvailabilityState
| Quantity | Rule | The measurable amount of a Product available in the Inventory | Count, Level, Amount
| Unit | Rule | The measurement type used for a Product`s Quantity (e.g., pieces, liters, pounds) | MeasurementUnit
| Consumption | Event | The act of using or reducing an Product`s Quantity over time | Usage, Depletion
| Purchase | Action | The act of buying a Product to increase its Quantity in the Inventory | Restock, Buy
| Shopping List | Entity | A temporary list of Products that need to be purchased soon | GroceryList, PurchaseList
| Supplier | Entity | A store or provider where a Product can be purchased or restocked from | Store, Vendor
| Restock Threshold | Rule | The minimum Quantity at which a Product should be replenished | MinimumStock, LowStockLimit
| Product Duplicate | Rule | Two records that refer to the same Product definition, identified by matching barcode or normalized name + brand + size + variant |
| Stock Duplicate | Rule | Two Stock entries for the same Product, same owner, same location, and purchase timestamps within 2 minutes — indicating an accidental double-entry |
| Membership | Entity | The association between a Stakeholder and a Home, carrying a Role and a status (active, pending, removed) |
| Invitation | Entity | A pending request sent to a Stakeholder to join a Home as a Member; has statuses: pending, accepted, declined, expired, revoked |
| Restock Request | Action | A request created to indicate a Product needs to be purchased or refilled | ShoppingTask, RefillRequest
| Budget | Entity | A resource allocation plan/rule-set which divides amount of money into Categories to allow for specific amount of Purchases in those Categories |
| Near-Duplicate | Rule | An item similar enough to an existing Product to trigger a duplicate warning, but not an authoritative match. Examples: same product name with different size, same brand with a slightly different variant. Requires explicit user confirmation before reuse. | SimilarItem, PotentialDuplicate
| Spend Event | Entity | An immutable record of a purchase action: `{user_id, household_id, product_id, amount, date, source, optional receipt_id}`. Spend events must not be merged or deleted as a side-effect of inventory changes. | PurchaseRecord, Transaction
| Allocation | Rule | How a Spend Event is attributed — either as a shared budget split (equal, weighted, or custom) or as an individual attribution to the buyer only. | Attribution, BudgetSplit
|===
