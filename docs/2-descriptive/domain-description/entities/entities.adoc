== Entities

Key domain entities (nouns) and their attributes.

[cols="2,4,4",options="header"]
|===
|Entity |Description |Key attributes
| Home | A physical space where people live and where a household inventory is managed. | name, rooms, inventory, stakeholders
| Room | A specific area within a Home where Stock can be stored (e.g., kitchen, bedroom, pantry, garage). | name, purpose (optional)
| Product | A household item definition that is commonly restocked (e.g., milk, toilet paper, dish soap). This represents the item concept independent of a specific purchase. | name, category (optional), brand (optional), size (optional), variant (optional), unit (optional), supplier (optional)
| Stock | An instance of a Product that represents a specific purchased item tracked in the inventory (e.g., two cartons of milk tracked separately by expiration date and location). | product, quantity, expiration date (optional), purchase details (optional), status, owner, notes (optional)
| Inventory | The collection of Products and Stock in a Home, representing what is currently available. | list of stock, product summaries, status summaries
| Stock Location | The association between a Stock item and the Room where it is stored. | stock, room
| Stakeholder | A person who has a vested interest in a Home and its Inventory. Stakeholders interact with the system according to assigned Roles. | name, associated home(s), role(s)
| Owner | A Stakeholder role representing the primary manager of a Home who has the highest access and permission to make changes. | permissions, managed home(s)
| Member | A Stakeholder role representing a person who lives in a Home and should have access to interact with its Inventory. | permissions, associated home(s)
| Guest | A Stakeholder role representing a person who has view-only or limited access to a Home and its Inventory. | permissions, associated home(s)
| Category | A grouping label used to organize Products by type or purpose (e.g., food, cleaning, electronics). | name, description (optional)
| Shopping List | A temporary list of Products that need to be purchased soon. | list of products, quantities (optional), priority (optional)
| Supplier | A store or provider where a Product can be purchased or restocked (e.g., Walmart, Costco, CVS). | name, location (optional), notes (optional)
| Budget | A plan that allocates money across Categories to support household purchasing decisions. | amount, categories, time period (optional), limits (optional)
|===

Note:
- Owner, Member, and Guest represent role types assigned to a Stakeholder within a Home context.
