== Entities

Key domain entities (nouns) and their attributes. These entities describe the domain independently of any specific system implementation.

[cols="2,4,4",options="header"]
|===
|Entity |Description |Key attributes
| Home | A physical space where people live and where a household inventory is managed. | name, rooms, inventory, stakeholders
| Room | A specific area within a Home where Stock can be stored (e.g., kitchen, bedroom, pantry, garage). | name, purpose (optional)
| Product | A household item definition that is commonly restocked (e.g., milk, toilet paper, dish soap). This represents the item concept independent of a specific purchase. | name, description, tags, unit (optional), image (optional)
| Stock | An instance of a Product that represents a specific purchased item tracked in the inventory (e.g., two cartons of milk tracked separately by expiration date and location). | brand, quantity, status, expiration date (optional)
| Inventory | The collection of Products and Stock in a Home, representing what is currently available. | reference to owner, stock registry
| Stock Location | The association between a Stock item and the Room where it is stored. | stock, room
| Stakeholder | A person who has a vested interest in a Home and its Inventory. Stakeholders interact with the system according to assigned Roles. | name, associated home(s), role(s)
| Owner | A Stakeholder role representing the primary manager of a Home who has the highest access and permission to make changes. | permissions, managed home(s)
| Member | A Stakeholder role representing a person who lives in a Home and should have access to interact with its Inventory. | permissions, associated home(s)
| Guest | A Stakeholder role representing a person who has view-only or limited access to a Home and its Inventory. | permissions, associated home(s)
| Category | A grouping label used to organize Products by type or purpose (e.g., food, cleaning, electronics). | name, description (optional)
| Shopping List | A temporary list of Products that need to be purchased soon. | list of products, quantities (optional), priority (optional)
| Supplier | A store or provider where a Product can be purchased or restocked (e.g., Walmart, Costco, CVS). | name, location (optional), notes (optional)
| Budget | A plan that allocates money across Categories to support household purchasing decisions. | amount, categories, time period (optional), limits (optional)


|Household
|A living unit where household essentials and spending are managed.
|name (optional label), members, locations, inventory (reality), optional budgets

|Household member
|A person who belongs to a household and consumes/replenishes items.
|name/identifier (domain-level), household reference, role (informal)

|Location
|A storage place within a household where items are kept.
|name/label, household reference, optional purpose

|Item type
|A definition of an item independent of a specific package/purchase.
|name, optional category, optional brand/size/variant, optional barcode

|Item instance (package)
|A concrete package/unit of an item type that exists in the household.
|item type reference, location reference, ownership (personal/shared), quantity/availability, optional purchase date, optional expiration date, optional notes

|Tracked inventory record
|A representation households use to reason about availability (may diverge from reality).
|artifact type (note/chat/list/memory), item reference (explicit or implicit), last-updated (if recorded)

|Restock list/planner
|A list of items judged to need replenishment soon.
|entries (item types), reason (low/out/expired), optional responsibility/intent marker, status (active/deferred)

|Alert / notification record
|A record of an alert-worthy condition presented to household members.
|type (low/out/near-expiry/expired/budget), target (item type/instance or budget), created time, read/unread, dismissed/deferred

|Spending entry
|A logged financial transaction event within a household.
|amount, date/time, payer (member), scope (shared/personal), optional item type reference, category, notes, transaction type (purchase/refund/adjustment)

|Budget
|A spending limit defined for a period.
|scope (household/category), limit amount, cycle (period), start/end (or recurrence), thresholds (warning levels), visibility rules

|Budget status (derived)
|A derived view of spending vs budget within a cycle.
|spent-to-date, remaining, percent used, threshold-crossed flags
|===
